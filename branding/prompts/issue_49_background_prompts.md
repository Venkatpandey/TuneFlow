## Splash / Startup Background

Use case: stylized-concept  
Asset type: branded TV app splash background  
Primary request: Dark cinematic background for a premium music streaming TV interface. Soft gradient field, subtle flowing wave patterns, low visual noise, restrained glow, minimal composition, elegant and modern, no text, no clutter, designed for overlay UI readability.  
Scene/backdrop: abstract atmospheric dark space with gentle flowing wave forms  
Style/medium: premium digital abstract background art  
Composition/framing: wide 16:9 composition, center-weighted but calm, clear negative space for UI overlay  
Lighting/mood: low-key cinematic lighting, restrained glow, soft radial depth  
Color palette: deep charcoal, graphite, near-black, restrained purple accent only  
Materials/textures: smooth gradients, faint ribbon waves, no grain, no particles  
Constraints: no text, no logos, no mockup framing, no device frame, no excessive glow, no neon overload, no dense detail, no bright focal clutter

## Hero Background

Use case: stylized-concept  
Asset type: TV app home hero background  
Primary request: Premium abstract hero background for a music streaming application. Deep dark palette, subtle motion-inspired ribbons, soft purple accent restraint, elegant, minimal, cinematic, low detail density, suitable for TV UI, no text, no bright focal clutter.  
Scene/backdrop: dark wide hero field with gentle layered ribbons and broad negative space for title and metadata overlay  
Style/medium: premium abstract digital background  
Composition/framing: 16:9 landscape, quieter left half for text, richer ribbon motion on right half, still minimal overall  
Lighting/mood: moody, cinematic, soft restrained highlights only  
Color palette: deep charcoal, aubergine shadows, restrained purple accent, almost-black base  
Materials/textures: satin-like ribbons, soft gradients, no particles, no noise  
Constraints: no text, no logos, no device frame, no mockup framing, no excessive glow, no neon overload, no dense detail

## Now Playing Fallback Background

Use case: stylized-concept  
Asset type: now playing fallback branded background  
Primary request: dark supporting background for a premium TV music player used only when album art is unavailable. Must remain subordinate to album art normally, must not reduce readability of title, artist, progress, or controls.  
Scene/backdrop: abstract dark field with very soft blurred flowing geometry and gentle radial depth  
Style/medium: minimal cinematic digital background  
Composition/framing: 16:9 landscape, low-contrast center, subtle edge vignetting, calm overall  
Lighting/mood: understated, deep, atmospheric, low-glow  
Color palette: near-black, charcoal, muted violet restraint, no bright accents  
Materials/textures: soft blur-like ribbons, smooth gradients, no pattern noise, no particles  
Constraints: no text, no logos, no bright focal clutter, no excessive glow, no neon overload, no dense detail, designed for UI readability
